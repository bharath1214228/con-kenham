Property refurbishment London

Kenham offer end-to-end property refurbishment. London based but also servicing Surrey, Berkshire and Buckinghamshire, Kenham's expert team can deal with everything from planning and design to construction and finishing.

Our specialism is in realising your vision, and creating something that makes you proud. We transform properties into spectacular talking points

We excel when it comes to overcoming challenges, such as the space limitations which are typical for London house refurbishment. The standard of our workmanship is very high end, and our reputation for reliability and flexibility is excellent. It's why people have been coming to us to refurbish their homes for over 20 years.

People are at the heart of what we do, and we take pride in providing a hassle free experience so you can sit back and enjoy the process.
